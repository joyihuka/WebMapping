About the Book!

"Dreams of a Scientist: Lessons from the Sea" is a children’s picture book that aims to educate kids and their support systems about the ocean by highlighting the work of 20 active scientists from historically excluded groups. We want every kid, no matter their background or identity, to feel empowered by these scientists to turn curiosity into discovery and their dreams into a reality.
